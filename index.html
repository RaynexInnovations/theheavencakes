Information Desk

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about baking details, custom shapes, or deliveries? Find quick answers here.

Do you make custom cakes?

Yes, we specialize in high-end customized cakes for weddings, anniversaries, corporate launches, and kids birthday parties. You can send us reference sketches, text messages, or photos, and our head baker will construct a custom recipe and design proposal for you.

Do you deliver in Kumbla?

Yes, we provide door-step cake shipping across Kumbla and adjacent parts of Kasaragod district. All deliveries are made using temperature-controlled cake carriers to assure structural stability and maximum freshness on arrival.

How early should I place an order?

For standard featured cakes, ordering 4 to 6 hours in advance is perfect. For luxury bespoke wedding cakes or multi-tiered complex configurations, we recommend booking your order at least 2 to 3 days in advance to secure customized elements.

Do you make eggless and healthy cakes?

Yes! We offer 100% vegetarian eggless options for all our cake flavors. We also make gluten-free and reduced-sugar cakes upon request. Simply check the eggless preference box or mention it while placing custom orders.
